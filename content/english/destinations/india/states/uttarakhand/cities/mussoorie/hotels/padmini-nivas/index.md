This friendly, old-fashioned, small hotel has an ideal location just below the Mall road and a wonderful Colonial atmosphere. There are lovely gardens, with pleasant views, comfortable rooms, and delicious vegetarian food is served in the grand, high-ceilinged dining room. The restaurant offers possibly the best food in town - wholesome vegetarian food served in a high ceilinged dining room.
