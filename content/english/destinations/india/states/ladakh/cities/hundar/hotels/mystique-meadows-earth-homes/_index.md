Mystique Meadows is a collection of spacious, comfortable mud cottages in a pleasant, terraced garden, with gorgeous views of the Karakoram range. Close to Diskit and the dunes, this offers a warm stay option in the Nubra valley. Apart from the indoor restaurant, the hotel has an outdoor lounge area, and can arrange private dinners under the humongous skies of Ladakh.
