In the Nubra Valley, within the Hundar village, lies the Sarai camp, surrounded by barren, snowy mountains near the sand dunes and the camel ride area. In between the gardens outside runs a stream of crystal- clear water. Accommodation is in comfortable octagonal tents with permanent floors and bathrooms, and Western and Ladakhi cuisine are available.
