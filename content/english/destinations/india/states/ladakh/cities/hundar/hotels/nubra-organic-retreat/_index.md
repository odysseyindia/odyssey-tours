Nubra Organic Retreat is situated in the beautiful village of Hundar, just 7km from Diskit, the headquarters of the Nubra valley, and two minutes walk from the famous sand dunes. Spread over 5 acres of lush green farm land and orchards, the retreat boasts of 22 double-bedded tents. It has its own organic garden, and guests can enjoy the taste of locally-grown vegetables.
