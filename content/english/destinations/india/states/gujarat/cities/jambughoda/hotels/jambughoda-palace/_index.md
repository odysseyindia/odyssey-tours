Jambughoda's palace is run as a home for nature lovers by the family, who still live in part of it. The 20 rooms, some air-conditioned and all featuring charming antiques, are a comfortable base from which to explore the Jambughoda wildlife sanctuary. It's a pleasure to return to the palace after a game drive and discuss sightings and bygone days with fellow guests and the gracious royal family.
