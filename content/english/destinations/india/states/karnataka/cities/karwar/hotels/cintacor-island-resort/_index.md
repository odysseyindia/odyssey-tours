A short boat ride from the busy port city of Karwar gets you to this lovely retreat, with its airy cottages and total seclusion. The cabins are all spacious, and all offer views of the sea. There is an infinity pool, a restaurant, a casual bar and a lounge with a library, as if having an island largely to yourself wasn't enough. A very different side to India, and a great end to a trip.
