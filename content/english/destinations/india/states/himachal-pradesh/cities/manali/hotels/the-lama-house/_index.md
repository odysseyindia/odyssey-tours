Run by an adventure-travel junkie, The Lama House is a house originally built by a Buddhist lama, as the name suggests. Now a cosy homestay, it encourages conscious living amid nature. There's a library, lots of restful spots in the garden, a coffee lounge to help you keep warm, and the dining room offers (there's a story!) Mediterranean food, including wood-fired pizzas!
