Hotel Broadway is a mid-range hotel straddling New and Old Delhi. In fact, it is very close to the main sights of Old Delhi such as the Red Fort, the Jama Masjid Mosque and Chandni Chowk bazaar. The hotel is known for the "Chor Bizarre" restaurant, which serves formidable Mughlai and Kashmiri fare in quirky surroundings - there's a vintage car salad bar! The "Thugs" bar serves just about anything you can think of.
