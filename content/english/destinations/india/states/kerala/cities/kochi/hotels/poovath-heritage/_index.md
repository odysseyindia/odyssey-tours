The Poovath Heritage Hotel is a renovated century-old Dutch Palace, located at Fort Cochin next to the Dutch Cemetery. It is stylish boutique hotel with lovely views over the Arabian Sea.    The hotel has 10 spacious and well appointed rooms that retain the colonial ambience. There is a swimming pool in the garden. The restaurant serves Thai, North and South Indian, Continental, Italian and Chinese cuisine. There is a swimming pool in the garden.
