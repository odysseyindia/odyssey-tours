Ashok Country Resort is set in sprawling green grounds close to Delhi's international airport. The sense of space and quiet is amazing considering the proximity to the airport. The rooms are old-fashioned but spacious and comfortable, with soft beds and pleasant furnishings. There is a lovely pool in the hotel gardens, and a library bar, making this the perfect place to relax before or after a long flight.
