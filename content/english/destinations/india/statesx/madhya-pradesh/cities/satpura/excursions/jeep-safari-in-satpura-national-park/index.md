Your stay in Satpura is on Jungle Plan basis, which includes all meals, and one shared jeep safari with a naturalist per day. Each ride takes you into heart of the wilderness. You also have the choice of one other jungle activity per day - you can choose between a night safari, nature walk, canoe ride or a boat ride.
