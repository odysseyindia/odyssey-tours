An unusual hotel offering spacious apartments, Om Niwas is located in a leafy residential area not far from Jaipur's attractions. A sister hostelry of the famous Arya Niwas, Om Niwas is a comfortable and aesthetically pleasing hotel, with self-contained apartments. A major attraction here is the terrace garden with a carefully tended lawn and a riot of flowering plants in neat rows.
